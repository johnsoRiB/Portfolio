Soccer in the Pacific Northwest

An Interactive Web Map

This is an interactive web map that details the history of the three major soccer teams in the Pacific Northwest, the Seattle Sounders, Vancouver Whitecaps, Portland Timbers, and how they have influenced soccer in the area. This web map explores how they interact with each other, the growth of women's soccer in the United States, and the current status of soccer in the Pacific Northwest.!
